# Interview Me

What people ask for and what they actually want are different things. Close the gap before any spec/code exists.

## Process
1. Hypothesize with confidence number (0-100%)
2. Ask one question at a time, each with a guess attached
3. Listen for "want vs should want" (buzzwords as goals, deferring to convention)
4. Restate intent: Outcome / User / Why now / Success / Constraint / Out of scope
5. Confirm — explicit yes required, not "whatever you think"

## Stop: Can you predict user's reaction to next 3 questions? If yes, done.

## Output: Confirmed statement of intent → handoff to idea-refine or spec-driven-development
